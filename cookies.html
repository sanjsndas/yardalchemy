🍪 Cookies Policy · Digital Seed Ethics

Minimal, transparent, and always in service to your experience. Just like companion planting, we place only what's beneficial — no invasive tracking, no hidden harvest.

🌾 Last Amended: April 15, 2025 · Rooted version 2.1

At YardAlchemy Botanicals, we believe that digital consent should be as clear as asking a fellow gardener before stepping into their sacred space. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, how they support your journey, and how you can manage your preferences. Our goal: nourish your visit without overharvesting your data.

🌿 1. What Are Cookies? (The Tiny Plant Analogies)

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. Think of them as little biodegradable markers — like a herbal tag that helps us remember your preferences or understand how our digital garden grows. They are not harmful, and they cannot carry viruses or install software. We use only first-party and strictly vetted anonymous cookies.

🌻 Our philosophy: No third-party tracking cookies for advertising. No ‘evercookies’. No selling of cookie data. We treat each cookie like a seed — only sow what is necessary and respectful.

🌱 2. Types of Cookies We Nurture

To keep YardAlchemy Botanicals thriving and user-friendly, we separate our cookies into three essential categories — each with a clear purpose:

Cookie FamilyPurposeExamples & Lifespan
🌿 Essential (Strictly Necessary) Required for core site functionality: form submissions, cart operations (if applicable), security checks, and session management. Cannot be disabled without breaking the experience. session_id, csrf_token. Expire when you close browser or within a few hours.
🍃 Preference & Functional Remember your choices such as language preference, region, or if you've dismissed a notification. Enhances convenience. user_lang, region_pref. Persistent up to 12 months.
📊 Anonymized Analytics Help us understand aggregate traffic patterns, popular pages, and user flow — all via privacy-forward analytics (IP anonymized, no cross-site tracking). Improves our content just like crop rotation. _ga (anonymized), _gid (first-party, aggregated). Lifespan: 24 hours to 2 years, but no personal identifiers.

Note: We do NOT deploy advertising cookies, social media trackers, or any fingerprinting technologies. Your digital footprint remains yours.

🍂 3. How We Use Cookie Information

Information collected via cookies helps us tend to our botanical web presence:

  • Ensure the website works reliably (keep you logged in during checkout for herbal orders).
  • Analyze which plant remedies or workshops are most engaging — so we can create more relevant content.
  • Detect and prevent security issues (like bots attempting to abuse forms).
  • Remember your cookie preferences for future visits, avoiding repeated consent banners (when you’ve already chosen).

All analytics data is aggregated and never tied back to your name, email, or specific identity. Our analytics provider processes data under strict data protection terms and cannot resell any information.

🌾 4. Your Consent & Cookie Management (Digital Sovereignty)

When you first visit YardAlchemy Botanicals, you will see a small cookie banner asking for your permission — except for strictly necessary cookies that keep the site operational. You can customize your preferences anytime using the "Cookie Preferences" button below or via our floating consent tool. Granting consent is optional, and you can withdraw it without any penalty.

🍪 Manage Your Cookie Preferences Instantly

Choose which categories to enable (excluding essential cookies which are always active for site safety).

Your choice will be stored for 12 months. You can change your mind anytime — we respect every leaf of your choice.

You can also disable cookies via your browser settings (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari). However, disabling essential cookies may cause parts of the site to malfunction (like booking forms or login areas).

🌼 5. Third-Party Services & Embedded Content

We occasionally embed content from trusted partners — such as a YouTube video about herbal distillation or an open source calendar for workshops. These third parties may set their own cookies if you interact with the embedded content. However, we do not load trackers without your explicit interaction (no automatic tracking). We vet all third-parties to ensure they align with our ethical data pledge.

  • Payment gateways: Stripe / PayPal may set cookies during checkout — these are essential for transaction security but are strictly limited.
  • Open source tools: Our contact forms and email signup are cookie-light (no cross-device tracking).

If you have concerns, we recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those third parties. YardAlchemy Botanicals is not responsible for external cookie practices, but we select partners with care.

🌻 6. Cookie Lifespan & Data Retention (Seasonal Cycles)

Our cookies respect natural cycles:

  • Session cookies expire when you close your browser — ephemeral like morning dew.
  • Persistent cookies remain for up to 12 months, after which they automatically expire or renew based on renewed consent. We never use permanent tracking mechanisms.
  • Analytics identifiers are anonymized and rolled up every 14 months; raw logs are deleted regularly.
🕊️ Transparency first: You can always clear cookies via your browser settings at any time. For more guidance, visit aboutcookies.org. Should you wish to erase any cookie data we store server-side (like preferences), just email privacy@yardalchemybotanicals.com.

🍃 7. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Our website honors Do Not Track browser settings. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we automatically disable all non-essential cookies and analytics — leaving only strictly necessary ones. We believe in aligning technology with user intent. You can enable DNT in your browser settings (often found under privacy/security).

📜 8. Updates to This Cookies Policy

As our digital garden evolves, this policy may be refreshed to reflect new practices or regulatory updates. If we make material changes (e.g., introducing new cookie types), we will notify you via a prominent banner or email alert at least 14 days in advance. The "Last Amended" date at the top always indicates the latest version. Your continued use after updates implies acceptance of the revised policy unless you withdraw consent.

🌲 9. Children’s Privacy & Cookies

We do not knowingly use cookies to track children under 13. Any analytics that could involve younger visitors is fully anonymized. If a parent or guardian has concerns about cookie storage related to a child, please contact us, and we will take appropriate measures to delete any residual data.
