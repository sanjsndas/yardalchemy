🌼 Roots & Inquiries — FAQs

🌿 Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely! Our garden alchemists conduct zoom sessions for permaculture design, soil coaching, and garden mapping worldwide. Just select “consultation” in the form.

🌻 Can I volunteer in the nursery?

Yes — we host “Green Hands” volunteer days every first Saturday. Help with seed starting, composting, and plant propagation. Send a message to join our guild!

🍃 Do you ship seeds and plants?

We ship heirloom seeds and dried herbs nationwide (USA). Live plants local pickup only. Check our online shop or email for seasonal availability.

📚 What workshops are coming?

“Mushroom Log Inoculation” on April 12, “Herbal Salve Making” on April 26, and “No-Dig Revolution” May 10. Subscribe to our newsletter via contact form!

🌿 Need quick plant diagnosis or garden emergency? Join our live “Plant ER” Instagram session every Tuesday 4pm EST — or drop an email with images to plantclinic@yardalchemybotanicals.com. We read every leaf signal.
